Website Checklist

Developing a website is a challenging operation that, in order to be carried out well, calls for careful organization and direction. Companies have a tendency to lose potential clients when their websites are not designed to be user-friendly and are not easily accessible to the customers they are targeting (Rathore, Ilavarasan and Dwivedi, 2016). Because of the rapid development of technology, every business now uses some form of digital communication to maintain connections with their respective target audiences. Accessibility, identity, navigation, and content are the website checklists that a corporation needs to keep in mind while they are constructing a website.

After laying down the mission and objectives of the website, the web designer will need to come up with a domain name for the website and register it. It is necessary to compile an accurate list of requirements, which should include the required quantity of website storage, the number of video and audio players, a shopping cart, and technical support (Rathore, Ilavarasan and Dwivedi, 2016). After careful consideration has been given to the building of the website, it is necessary for it to be disseminated to the general public by means of social media, social cards, advertising, and directories.

The categories and services on the website of online retailer Amazon, where they have been extremely clearly identified, serve as a good example of this. If Amazon is going to be rated using the previously described checklist, it is feasible that it will receive a good rating if accessibility and other criteria like identity, content, and navigation have been established correctly. The brand’s logo is placed correctly, and the search and color options are also proper. Additionally, pop-up windows and adverts are shown on the website in a way that is completely undetectable. The relevant symbols are used when displaying numbers on screens, such as those found in shopping carts where users can save their wish lists for later purchases. Additionally, the company details and a phone number are provided in a separate area, making it easier to contact the proper authority in the event of a problem. Due to the fact that Amazon is an online shop, the business uses eye-catching imagery and carefully chosen phrasing to draw potential customers to its most recent goods. For instance, the promotion of the laptop on the website is done incredibly successfully with the help of suitable images and discount deals that catch website users’ attention. When a valid log in id has been created, existing customers can quickly shop online by logging into their accounts, which can be located on the right-hand side of the webpage.
